# Additional Engineering Best Practices (Python)

## Structural Discipline

- Avoid global mutable state
- Make data flow explicit
- Separate pure logic from side effects

---

## Testing & Maintainability

- Functions SHOULD be testable in isolation
- Avoid tight coupling between modules

---

## Long-Term Maintainability

- Do NOT rely on dynamic monkey-patching
- Avoid metaprogramming unless strictly necessary
- Favor clarity over Python-specific tricks
